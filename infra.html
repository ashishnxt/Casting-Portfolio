WHAT WE DO


At Royal Casting, we specialize in the precision manufacturing of steel and cast iron castings, offering a comprehensive range of solutions tailored to the unique needs of our clients.

Steel Casting

Our steel casting process involves melting high-quality steel alloys and pouring the molten metal into carefully crafted molds. This method allows us to produce intricate and robust components with exceptional strength and durability.

Cast Iron Casting

Our cast iron casting process combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. We melt and pour molten cast iron into molds, creating components known for their versatility, heat resistance, and exceptional machining properties.
